1. Find a movie from a description (classic use case)
"What is the movie where a hacker discovers that the world is a simulation?"
"I'm looking for a movie about a ship that hits an iceberg."
"What's the movie where a robot wants to become human?"
"Is there a movie where a man ages backwards?"
"What's the movie where people enter each other's dreams?"

2. Recommend movies based on a description
"Recommend movies about time travel."
"I want thought-provoking science fiction movies."
"Suggest movies about artificial intelligence."
"Recommend movies involving police investigations and serial killers."
"Suggest movies similar to stories about surviving on a deserted island."

3. Search by theme
"Movies about friendship."
"Movies that deal with grief."
"Movies that criticize capitalism."
"Movies about freedom."
"Movies that explore moral dilemmas."

These queries are interesting because many movie summaries may never use these exact words while still conveying the same themes.

4. Search by specific story elements
"Movies where the main character loses their memory."
"Movies where the protagonist is a detective."
"Movies about aliens invading Earth."
"Movies that take place in a prison."
"Movies set almost entirely on a train."

5. Search by mood
"I want a sad movie."
"I want a light-hearted movie to watch tonight."
"Movies that are tense from beginning to end."
"Movies with a surprising ending."
"Movies that leave the audience confused."

6. Search by combinations
"I want a romance with science fiction."
"Horror movies set in space."
"A comedy about time travel."
"Action movies with fantasy elements."
"Drama based on a true story."
7. Long natural language queries

These are the kinds of queries that really showcase the advantage of semantic search.

"I want to watch a movie where the main character discovers that everything they believed was a lie."
"I'm looking for a movie about an artificial intelligence that starts developing emotions."
"Recommend a movie similar to The Matrix, but not The Matrix itself."
"I want a movie that combines philosophy, action, and science fiction."
"I want a movie where a group of people must survive in a hostile environment."
